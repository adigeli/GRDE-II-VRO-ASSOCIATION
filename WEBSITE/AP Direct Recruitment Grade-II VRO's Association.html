About Our Association

The AP Direct Recruitment Grade-II VRO's Association, Ananthapuramu District, is a registered organization (Regd No. 113/2025) committed to supporting Village Revenue Officers. Our mission is to foster unity, address professional challenges, and promote the welfare of our members.
